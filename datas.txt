**Raise Smart School of Technology** is a premier tech-first upskilling and knowledge partner that works with accredited universities to deliver industry-aligned education alongside standard degrees. It is uniquely located inside the Rathinam IT and Technology Park in Coimbatore, providing students with direct daily exposure to over 50 active tech companies.

Here is a comprehensive overview of the data and offerings of Raise Smart:

**Educational Philosophy and Approach**
*   **Core Motto:** "Don’t Just Study Tech. Build It.".
*   **Competency-Based Learning:** Instead of traditional, theory-heavy education, Raise Smart emphasizes hands-on learning where students build real-world projects and solve actual problems from their very first semester.
*   **Industry Immersion:** Every semester includes structured industry immersion programs, live projects, company interactions, and mandatory skill certifications (like programming, AI, and cloud). 

**Key Facilities and Resources**
*   **Hardware Provided:** Every student is equipped with a high-performance **MacBook Neo** pre-configured with industry-standard tools.
*   **AI Skills Lab:** The campus features a state-of-the-art **AI Skills Lab inaugurated by Dell & Intel** for high-performance computing and real AI project development.
*   **Workspace:** Students work in a collaborative, corporate-style environment known as the **Peer Power Hive**.

**Academic Programs**
Raise Smart offers a variety of future-tech focused programs:
*   **B.E & B.Tech:** Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AIDS), Computer Science Engineering (CSE), CSE with AI/ML, and CSE with Cyber Security.
*   **BSc:** Programs include Computer Science with AI Certification by INTEL, Information Technology with Data Science, Visual Communication (AI ready animation & VFX), and Costume Design and Fashion.
*   **MBA:** Includes programs integrated with AI tools across disciplines. Top future specializations include Business Analytics & AI, Digital Marketing, FinTech, and AI Product Management.

**Student Development Platforms**
The institution heavily promotes peer-to-peer growth through the **Students for Students (S4S)** platform:
*   **CRAFT (Wing 1):** Focuses on technical competence and career readiness through hackathons and innovation challenges.
*   **TALKONAUTS (Wing 2):** Focuses on communication, public speaking, and leadership development.
*   **AITED:** A platform for students to explore Artificial Intelligence tools and emerging tech.
*   **Pro Power Talks:** Biweekly sessions where industry professionals share real-world insights with students.

**Placements and Career Outcomes**
*   **Top Salary Packages:** Graduates have secured packages up to **₹3 Crore per annum**, with multiple students placed in the ₹45 LPA to ₹58 LPA range.
*   **Career Readiness:** Placement preparation does not happen at the end of the degree; resume building, LinkedIn optimization, and mock interviews begin in semester one. 

**Admissions and Scholarships (RSTNAT)**
*   **Entrance Exam:** Admission and scholarships are determined by the **RSTNAT (Raise Smart National Aptitude Test)**, a 90-minute, 100-question exam.
*   **Exam Structure:** The Undergraduate exam covers Physics, Chemistry, Math, Tech Awareness, Aptitude, Communication, and Programming Basics with **no negative marking**. The MBA entrance exam covers Aptitude, Business, and Tech Awareness and **includes negative marking**.
*   **Scholarships:** Students can earn **up to a 100% full fee waiver** depending on their All India Rank (e.g., Ranks 1–50 get 100%, 51–200 get 75%, etc.).
